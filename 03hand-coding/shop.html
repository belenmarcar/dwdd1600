Shop

At our shop, you'll find everything you need to create the most delicious Ecuadorian dishes, right in the comfort of your home. We understand that sourcing authentic ingredients can be challenging, especially when you're not in Ecuador. That's why we're here to make it easier for you by offering a wide selection of high-quality products that are essential for crafting traditional Ecuadorian meals.

Whether you're craving a hearty ceviche, a flavorful hornado, or a comforting bowl of locro de papas, we’ve got all the spices, vegetables, grains, and meats you need to bring the true taste of Ecuador to your kitchen. We take pride in offering top-notch customer service and ensuring that all of our ingredients are as fresh and authentic as possible.

No matter where you are in the world, we are committed to delivering Ecuadorian flavors to your doorstep with fast, reliable international shipping. Our mission is to make Ecuadorian cuisine accessible to everyone, no matter the distance.

Experience the vibrant and rich culinary traditions of Ecuador today, and let us help you create unforgettable meals that will transport you straight to the heart of Ecuador.

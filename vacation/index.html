Welcome to Diablo Huma Adventures!

Your gateway to Ecuador’s most breathtaking landscapes, vibrant cultures, and thrilling adventures. Inspired by the spirit of the Diablo Huma, the powerful Andean symbol of duality and balance, we invite you to experience Ecuador in a way that blends adventure, authenticity, and deep cultural immersion.

Whether you dream of standing on the equator, trekking through misty cloud forests, exploring ancient Incan ruins, or diving into the biodiversity of the Galápagos, Diablo Huma Adventures is here to guide you on an unforgettable journey.

Who We Are

At Diablo Huma Adventures, we believe travel is more than just visiting new places—it’s about immersing yourself in stories, traditions, and experiences that stay with you for a lifetime. Founded by passionate Ecuadorians, we are a locally owned and operated travel company specializing in adventure, eco-tourism, and cultural experiences across Ecuador. From the heights of the Andes to the depths of the Amazon, we take pride in showcasing our country’s natural beauty and rich heritage in a way that respects and supports local communities.

Sustainability & Community Commitment

We collaborate with indigenous groups, support conservation efforts, and ensure that our adventures benefit local economies. By choosing us, you’re not just exploring Ecuador—you’re helping to preserve its culture and environment for future generations

Why Travel With Us?

Our trips are designed to bring you closer to the heart of Ecuador. Whether it’s staying with a Kichwa family in the Amazon, learning ancient weaving techniques in Otavalo, or sharing a meal with a local fisherman on the coast, you’ll experience Ecuador in ways most tourists never do. Our bilingual guides are passionate storytellers, historians, and nature lovers who will enrich your journey with fascinating insights, hidden gems, and unforgettable moments. No two travelers are alike. That’s why we customize our packages to match your interests, pace, and travel style. Whether you're seeking adrenaline-pumping adventure, cultural enrichment, or relaxation, we’ll design the perfect itinerary just for you. We are committed to minimizing our environmental impact by working with eco-lodges, reducing waste, and supporting conservation projects. We believe in leaving every place we visit better than we found it.

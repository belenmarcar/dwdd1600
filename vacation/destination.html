Destinations

Ecuador is a land of contrasts, where towering volcanoes, lush rainforests, pristine beaches, and enchanting islands all exist within a small but diverse country. Explore our stunning destinations:

Andean Highlands

Walk through the cobbled streets of Quito, one of the best-preserved colonial cities in South America. Visit the indigenous market in Otavalo, where centuries-old traditions live on. Hike breathtaking volcanoes like Cotopaxi and Chimborazo.

Amazon Rainforest

Navigate the winding rivers of the Amazon Basin and witness exotic wildlife, from pink dolphins to playful monkeys. Learn from indigenous communities about their deep connection with nature.

Galápagos Islands

Walk among blue-footed boobies, giant tortoises, and sea lions in one of the most unique ecosystems on Earth. Snorkel with marine iguanas and explore crystal-clear waters teeming with life.

Ecuadorian Coast

Discover hidden beaches, explore colonial-era towns like Manta and Puerto López, and enjoy fresh seafood while watching the sunset over the Pacific Ocean.

Cloud Forests & Hidden Gems

Visit the mystical town of Mindo, where waterfalls, butterfly farms, and chocolate tours await. Explore the untouched beauty of Ecuador’s lesser-known gems.
